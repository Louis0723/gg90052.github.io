Global Master of Business Administration Program

Founded in 1993, the College of Management carries on the motto of“Excellence, Thoroughness, Vision, Harmony and Innovation” of the Far Eastern Group and Yuan Ze University by establishing the GMBA program in 2019. Since its inception, the program has attracted both local and international students on a multicultural learning environment. With 80% of students coming from a foreign country, the program is poised to lead everyone on journey to become a globalized manager.

Conducted entirely in English, the GMBA program allows you to elect a concentration in Business Management, Leadership and Human Resources Management, Marketing, or International Business. The program emphasizes communication skills and cross cultural team management with the aim of producing managers with global view and mobility.
